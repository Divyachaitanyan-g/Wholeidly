Wholesome Idly

Idly, a staple breakfast item in South Indian cuisine, is a soft and fluffy steamed rice cake that is both nutritious and delicious. Made from a fermented batter of rice and black lentils, idly is rich in protein and easily digestible.

Traditionally enjoyed with coconut chutney, sambar, and a variety of spicy powders, idly is a favorite for all age groups. It’s not just food; it’s an experience of heritage, flavor, and health in every bite.
